# Privacy Policy for GeminEye

**Last Updated:** 2026-05-30

## 1. Information Collection and Use

GeminEye is a client-side browser extension designed to monitor and estimate token usage on the Gemini Web UI (`gemini.google.com`). 

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data, chat history, or prompts.**

All text analysis and token estimation is performed locally on your device within your browser. 

## 2. Local Storage

GeminEye uses `chrome.storage.local` to store aggregated token counts (hourly and daily usage) and the timestamps for rate-limit reset windows. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by any external servers or third parties.

## 3. Third-Party Services

GeminEye does not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, or telemetry services.

## 4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/RhythmicDias/GeminEye).
